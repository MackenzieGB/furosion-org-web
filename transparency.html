Governance

The organisation is formally represented by its statutory organ in accordance with Slovak law.

Operational decisions are made within the organisation’s leadership structure and reviewed to ensure accountability, consistency, and alignment with the organisation’s mission.

Internal coordination is handled by the leadership team responsible for planning, approving, and executing projects and activities.

Funding and Resources

Furosion o.z. is currently in an early stage of development and is in the process of establishing long-term funding structures.

The organisation plans to operate through a combination of donations, sponsorships, grants, and event-based contributions where applicable.

All funding is intended to directly support the organisation’s activities, event organisation, and operational needs.

Use of Funds

Funds and resources are used to support the organisation’s programs and activities, including:

– Event organisation and logistics
– Materials and supplies for participants
– Venue or space rental when required
– Organisational tools and equipment
– Administrative and professional services where necessary

Financial Transparency

Furosion o.z. is preparing to establish a transparent financial structure, including a dedicated bank account for organisational funds.

As the organisation develops, financial reporting and documentation will be made available to ensure transparency and accountability.

Commitment to Transparency

Furosion o.z. is committed to operating in a transparent, accountable, and responsible manner.

The organisation aims to provide clear information about its structure, activities, and use of resources to its community, partners, and supporters.
