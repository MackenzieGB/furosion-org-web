Who We Are

Furosion o.z. is a registered youth-led organisation based in Bratislava, Slovakia. The organisation focuses on planning and delivering structured community events and projects for people.

It was created to provide a safer, more organised environment where participants can take part in events, meet others, and engage in community activities without the barriers often present in larger or less structured spaces.

Mission

The mission of Furosion o.z. is to create a structured and accountable system for organising specifically focused events and community projects.

The organisation aims to support young people in building social connections, developing practical skills, and participating in safe, well-managed environments.

Activities

Furosion organises meetups, outings, and planned future projects such as themed events, conventions, and talent-based activities.

Most current activities take place in the Bratislava region, primarily in accessible public locations, with plans to expand into private venues and larger-scale organised events.

Community Focus

The organisation currently focuses on youth communities connected through internet-based interests and subcultures, while aiming to expand into broader youth audiences in future projects.

A key priority is ensuring that events remain accessible, safe, and welcoming, particularly for younger participants.

Meet the Team

The organisation is led by a small core team responsible for planning, coordination, and event execution. More members are involved across different activities and projects.
