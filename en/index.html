FUROSION

Furosion o.z. is a Bratislava-based youth-led nonprofit organisation that plans and delivers structured in-person events for young people, and people that are parts of different fandoms, focusing on safety, accessibility, and community building.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to create safe, structured environments where people can participate in community events, develop real-world skills, and build meaningful connections.

What We Do

Furosion organises fandom-focused in-person events in Bratislava and surrounding regions. These include structured meetups, themed outings, and coordinated group activities designed to provide a safe and accessible environment.

Participants can attend events, connect with others, and optionally contribute as volunteers, gaining experience in logistics, coordination, and event planning.

Who We Serve

Furosion o.z. primarily serves young people or people with specific interests in the Bratislava region and surrounding areas. Activities are designed for individuals interested in community participation, creative subcultures, and structured group events.

Our Programs

Furosion o.z. operates multiple structured youth initiatives that support safe, organised, and community-focused in-person meetups in Bratislava and surrounding areas.

Our activities include regular meetups, larger community events, workshops, and festival projects, each with clear structure and coordination for safe operation.
