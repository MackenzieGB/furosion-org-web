St. Patrick’s Day Meetup Recap

March 2026 · Event Recap

Furosion o.z. hosted a structured community meetup in Bratislava as part of its Furosion Outings program. The event focused on safe, organised social interaction and planned group activities.

Around 25 participants attended. The meetup began at a central meeting point before moving to a quieter outdoor location where activities were held.
