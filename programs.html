Furosion o.z. runs structured youth programs focused on safe, in-person community events in Bratislava and surrounding regions. These programs are designed to support social connection, organisation, and participation in group activities.

Furosion Outings

Furosion Outings is the organisation’s primary active program. It consists of structured meetups for young people, typically held in safe public or semi-private outdoor locations.

Each event is planned in advance and includes organised group activities, coordination points, and supervised structure to ensure safety and accessibility.

The program is primarily designed for individuals aged 10–18 in the Bratislava region, with participation open to those able to travel to event locations.

How Events Work

Events begin at a designated meeting point where participants are welcomed and organised.

Groups then move to a planned location where structured activities take place, including games, social interaction, and themed challenges.

Events are coordinated by Furosion team members to ensure safety, structure, and smooth execution.

Past Activities

Furosion has organised multiple themed community events, including seasonal meetups such as Halloween, Christmas, and St. Patrick’s Day.

Recent events have hosted over 25 participants and included structured group coordination and organised activities.

Future Programs

Furosion o.z. is expanding its program structure to include larger-scale community initiatives such as conventions, talent showcases, and expanded youth events.
